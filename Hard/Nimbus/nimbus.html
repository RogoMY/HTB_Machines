What is this?

Nimbus runs scheduled and on-demand background jobs across our worker fleet. Used internally for backups, ETL, report generation, and anything that needs to run on a timer or off the request path.

How do I submit a job?

Drop the YAML in our internal Git, then point the job submitter at the raw URL. Or paste the YAML directly for ad-hoc runs.

Need shell access on a worker?

Use SSH. Your SSH key needs to be approved by a DevOps lead. Ping marcus on Slack.
